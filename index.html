About

Keegan's Furniture provides custom made furniture of remarkable quality for your home or business. Specializing in tables, wardrobes and fitted shelves, but also offering signs, and other solutions for your small business. Keegan's furniture is a small family run business, bourne out of passion for creation and a keen eye for detail. All materials are locally sourced and chosen specifically for each project. We look forward to creating something uniquee and beautiful for you.
